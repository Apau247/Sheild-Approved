Accreditations & Trust Signals

We maintain active compliance with the world's most respected precious metals and security standards. These are not decorative badges — they are independently verified and regularly renewed.

LBMA Compliant

Full compliance with London Bullion Market Association "loco London" best practices for vault storage, bar quality, and chain of custody.

Lloyd's of London

Comprehensive all-risk insurance underwritten by Lloyd's syndicates. Coverage matches current market value, not just purchase price.

UL TL-30X6 Certified

Vault doors and wall assemblies tested by Underwriters Laboratories to withstand 30 minutes of sustained attack from all six sides.

EN 1143-1 Grade 10+

European Norm certification for resistance to burglary using explosives, thermal cutting tools, and diamond core drills.

IRS Approved (IRA)

Approved custodian for precious metals Individual Retirement Accounts. Bars meet IRS fineness standards and are held in segregated allocated storage.

Bureau Veritas Audited

Quarterly physical audits by Bureau Veritas including bar count, weight verification, and serial number reconciliation against client records.

ISO 27001 Certified

Information security management system certified for protection of digital client records, access logs, and operational data.

EBS Member

Member of the European Bullion Storage Association, adhering to uniform standards for vault construction, insurance, and client disclosure.

Physical & Operational Security

Our London facility is built to withstand attack from every vector. Every specification below is independently certified, documented, and available for client review under NDA.

Reinforced Construction

  • Steel-reinforced concrete walls, floors, and ceilings — minimum 600mm composite thickness
  • Vault door: 90-ton mass with 24 steel locking bolts, time locks, and airtight seals
  • UL TL-30X6 certified — withstands 30 minutes of tool, torch, and explosive attack on all six faces
  • EN 1143-1 Grade 10+ rating — tested against diamond core drills, thermal cutting, and explosives
  • Seismic isolation foundation — decoupled from building structure to resist ground shock
  • Radio-frequency shielding (Faraday cage) around vault perimeter

Access Controls

  • Biometric entry: fingerprint, iris recognition, and 3D facial scan required simultaneously
  • Multi-factor authentication (MFA) at every access point — no single credential sufficient
  • Dual-control policy: two authorised personnel required for vault entry; no solo access
  • Mantrap airlocks between outer perimeter and vault core — sequential door control
  • Strict visitor protocols: escorted-only, pre-registered, ID verified against government databases
  • Time-locked doors with programmable delay (15–60 minutes) preventing forced rapid entry

Surveillance & Monitoring

  • 24/7 CCTV coverage inside and outside — 4K resolution, 90-day retention, off-site backup
  • Motion detectors with zoned sensitivity throughout facility perimeter and vault corridors
  • Seismic and tamper sensors on vault walls, doors, and flooring — real-time vibration analysis
  • Sound detection: anomaly recognition for drilling, cutting, or explosive discharge
  • Thermal imaging cameras detecting concealed heat signatures and unauthorised body heat
  • Off-site monitoring centre with armed rapid-response team on 4-minute dispatch contract

Environmental Controls

  • Climate-controlled storage: stable 18–22°C temperature, 35–45% relative humidity
  • Prevents tarnishing of silver, oxidation of gold, and degradation of gemological certificates
  • FM-200 clean-agent fire suppression — no water damage to stored assets
  • Flood protection: elevated vault floor (1.2m above street level), sump pumps, watertight seals
  • Redundant power: dual-grid connection + on-site diesel generators (72-hour autonomy)
  • Disaster-resistant systems: EMP shielding, blast-resistant outer shell, biological containment

Location & Perimeter

  • Secure low-profile industrial location — no external signage identifying vault contents
  • Perimeter fencing: 4.5m anti-climb mesh with anti-ram bollards and vehicle barriers
  • Metal detectors and X-ray screening for all personnel and packages entering secure zone
  • Integrated with Metropolitan Police and City of London Police for rapid incident response
  • Anonymous exterior — building appears as standard commercial logistics facility
  • Secure parking with blast-resistant barriers and vehicle inspection protocols

Employee Screening

  • Enhanced Disclosure and Barring Service (DBS) checks for all staff — renewed every 2 years
  • Financial probity screening: credit checks and conflict-of-interest declarations
  • Fidelity bonding: all personnel handling client assets covered by £5M fidelity insurance
  • Compartmentalised access: no single employee has knowledge of full security architecture
  • Continuous monitoring: random drug testing, behavioural analytics, and whistleblower channels
  • Mandatory security training: quarterly drills, social engineering resistance, incident response

Allocated & Segregated Storage

We never commingle client assets. Every bar, coin, and gemstone is individually identified, weighed, photographed, and assigned to your specific account.

What Allocated Means

  • Your specific bars and coins remain physically separate from all other client holdings
  • Each item receives a unique serial number recorded in your name on our ledger and the blockchain
  • Weight and purity verified by independent assay laboratory — certificate provided to you
  • Photographic documentation of every item at intake — high-resolution, tamper-evident watermark
  • No "unallocated" or pool storage: you own identifiable physical metal, not a fractional claim
  • Withdrawal: you receive the exact same bars you deposited, verified by serial number match

Proof of Ownership

  • Certificate of Storage issued within 24 hours of intake — includes serials, weights, assay refs
  • Quarterly statements with current market valuation and photographic re-verification
  • Online portal showing real-time inventory, audit status, and insurance coverage
  • Legal title remains with you at all times — we act solely as bailee, never as owner
  • Third-party verification: Bureau Veritas confirms your holdings match our records every quarter
  • Blockchain-anchored audit trail: every movement (intake, audit, transfer, withdrawal) is cryptographically timestamped

Insurance Coverage

Insurance is only as good as the underwriter and the policy wording. We use Lloyd's of London syndicates with A+ ratings, and coverage is structured to pay current market value — not a depreciated purchase price.

All-Risk Coverage

  • Theft: coverage for burglary, armed robbery, insider theft, and cyber-enabled theft
  • Loss: mysterious disappearance, transit loss, and documentation error recovery
  • Damage: fire, flood, earthquake, explosion, and accidental damage during handling
  • Transit: door-to-door coverage including air, sea, and armoured road transport worldwide
  • Terrorism and political violence: included as standard, not as a costly add-on
  • No sub-limits on gold or diamonds — full value covered without category caps

Market-Value Replacement

  • Valuation updated daily against LBMA AM/PM fixings — no stale pricing
  • Claims paid in your choice of currency: GBP, USD, EUR, CHF, or physical metal replacement
  • Lloyd's syndicate financial strength: A+ (Superior) rating from A.M. Best
  • Excess layer: £50M catastrophic coverage through reinsurance with Swiss Re
  • Policy wording available for client review — no hidden exclusions or wear-and-tear clauses
  • Claims response: 48-hour acknowledgement, 14-day settlement for verified losses

Independent Audits

We do not audit ourselves. Every physical count, weight check, and serial verification is performed by an independent third party with no commercial relationship to Iron Vault Security.

Quarterly

Physical Inventory Audit — Bureau Veritas
