Physical Security Specifications by Tier

Every tier meets or exceeds LBMA compliance standards. Upgrades are additive — Gold Service includes everything in Premium plus additional layers.

Standard — Essential Protection

  • Steel-reinforced vault compartment
  • Biometric fingerprint access
  • 24/7 CCTV with 30-day retention
  • Motion detection on corridors
  • Basic fire suppression
  • Climate control 18–22°C
  • Insurance: basic all-risk up to £250K
  • Audit: quarterly visual verification

Premium — Enhanced Protection

  • Private vault room with dual-key entry
  • Biometric + PIN authentication
  • 24/7 armed response on-site
  • Seismic and tamper sensors
  • FM-200 clean-agent fire suppression
  • Humidity control 35–45% RH
  • Insurance: enhanced up to £2M
  • Audit: monthly physical count

Gold Service — Maximum Protection

  • Custom vault suite, multi-factor access
  • Fingerprint + iris + facial recognition
  • 24/7 on-site armed response + rapid dispatch
  • Sound, thermal, and vibration analysis
  • Redundant power + blast-resistant shell
  • Faraday cage RF shielding
  • Insurance: Lloyd's comprehensive, unlimited
  • Audit: weekly verification + quarterly independent

LBMA Compliant — "Loco London" Standards: All precious metals stored at Iron Vault Security meet London Bullion Market Association best practices for vault construction, bar quality, chain of custody, and weight verification. Our facility is audited against LBMA guidelines annually.

Insurance Coverage & Audit Frequency

Insurance by Tier

Standard: Basic all-risk coverage up to £250,000 per compartment. Covers theft, fire, and accidental damage.

Premium: Enhanced coverage up to £2,000,000 per room. Includes transit, mysterious disappearance, and terrorism.

Gold: Comprehensive Lloyd's of London all-risk with no sub-limits. Market-value replacement, worldwide transit, and political violence included as standard.

Audit Frequency

Standard: Quarterly visual verification by internal vault officers. Serial numbers checked against ledger.

Premium: Monthly physical count + weight verification. Photographic documentation updated.

Gold: Weekly internal verification plus quarterly independent audits by Bureau Veritas — physical count, weight check, and serial reconciliation against client records.

Environmental Controls

All tiers include climate-controlled storage at 18–22°C and 35–45% relative humidity to prevent tarnishing, oxidation, and gemological certificate degradation.

Gold Service adds EMP shielding, blast-resistant outer shell, biological containment, and redundant power with 72-hour diesel generator autonomy.
