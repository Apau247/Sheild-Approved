Delivering Absolute Peace of Mind

At Iron Vault Security, we provide elite, high-security vault storage for precious minerals, gold, diamonds, and fine jewelry. Situated in the heart of London's financial district, our world-class facility was founded with a singular purpose: to offer discreet, dependable, and internationally accredited protection for your most valuable assets.

Our facility at 45 Bankside Lane, London EC2V 7NQ, UK features state-of-the-art biometric access control, 24/7 armed response teams, and climate-controlled environments that meet or exceed LBMA compliance standards.

From individual collectors to family offices and institutional clients, we tailor our protection tiers to match your specific needs — ensuring that what matters most to you remains absolutely secure.
